Introduction

Please carefully read and fill in the following application form for the Marsh Graduate Development Programme.

In accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998, you are requested to provide personal data, as set out in the form.

This information will be used to make recruitment decisions and for the purposes of equal opportunities assessment. We will use the information you provide on your ethnic background only to assist us in monitoring the diversity of our workforce.

If you agree, Marsh will keep the information you provide for 24 months in a secure database so that we can contact you if other opportunities arise during that period. After this time, your personal details will be deleted.

Thank you and good luck.
